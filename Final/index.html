ABOUT THEME

Bollywood has always made us dream about the rainbows and unicorns. But hey! Bollywood is practically incomplete without its iconic villains. Villains- they are scary, they are dark, they are evil and they are smart. Be it vibing to "Nayak nahi khalnayak hu mai'' to instantly saying "Mogambo khush hua", every time you're happy, speaks how villains have always had a special spot in our hearts. From utterly hating them back then, to understanding their point of view and supporting their characterization. Let's visit the Khalnayaks once again and open the evil's door. Aa jaao!
