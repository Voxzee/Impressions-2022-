ABOUT US

Impressions is an inter-collegiate annual media festival organized by BAMMC department of VES College of Arts, Science and Commerce (Autonomous). It is the longest running media festival in Mumbai. Impressions is the summation of incredible energy,fun and learning. Impressions provides the media students a platform to showcase their skills and talent while giving them a hands-on experience. The participants and guests are truly impressed by the phenomenal vibes of impressions. Every year, team impressions comes up with a unique theme which adds more essence and energy in the event. The dedication of our team makes us stand out to become one of the best media festivals and we are proud of it.

Bollywood is practically incomplete without its iconic villains. Villains- they are scary, they are dark, they are evil and they are smart. Let's sit back as you join us on this immensely nostalgic ride while we start the countdown of Revisiting these khalnayaks.. Aa jaao!
